1955-2011

Biography

Steve Jobs (1955-2011) co-founded Apple Inc. and left an indelible mark on the technology world. His vision and innovation led to iconic products like the Macintosh, iPod, iPhone, and iPad. Despite a brief departure from Apple in the mid-1980s, he returned to transform the company and set new industry standards. His emphasis on design and user experience reshaped consumer electronics. His captivating product launches and visionary thinking earned him a legendary status. Steve Jobs' legacy continues to inspire entrepreneurs and designers, making him a pivotal figure in the digital revolution. He passed away on October 5, 2011, but his impact endures.
